Khumai Dada

Khumai Dada is a scenic viewpoint in Nepal’s Lamjung District, offering panoramic views of the Annapurna and Manaslu ranges. It’s a tranquil spot ideal for trekking and enjoying nature, with picturesque landscapes and local cultural experiences. The best time to visit is during the spring and autumn. The Khumai Dada trek in Nepal provide a rich cultural experience.

Mardi Himal

Mardi Himal is a beautiful, off-the-beaten-path trek in Nepal's Annapurna region. It offers stunning views of the Annapurna and Machapuchare peaks. The trek is moderately challenging and usually takes 5-7 days. Best times to go are March-May or September-November. It provides a mix of lush forests, and charming village experiences.

Langtang

Langtang Himal is a stunning trek in Nepal's Langtang Valley, close to the Tibetan border. It offers breathtaking views of Langtang Lirung and other peaks, with diverse landscapes from lush forests to high-altitude meadows. The trek typically takes 7-10 days and is known for its scenic beauty and cultural richness.Langtang offers stunning peaks.

Ghale Gaun

Ghale Gaun is a picturesque traditional village in Nepal's Lamjung District, known for its beautiful views of the Annapurna and Manaslu ranges. It's a charming spot for experiencing authentic Gurung culture, with traditional houses and local hospitality. Ideal for a peaceful getaway, it’s best visited during the spring and autumn.

Panchpokhari

Panch Pokhari is a beautiful group of five sacred lakes nestled in the Himalayas, known for its stunning natural beauty and serene environment. It’s a popular trekking destination that offers breathtaking views and a peaceful atmosphere, attracting both pilgrims and nature lovers.The region around Panch Pokhari is rich in biodiversity, making it a haven for trekker.

Tilicho Lake

Tilicho Lake, is renowned for being one of the highest lakes in the world. Its striking turquoise waters are framed by majestic snow-capped peaks, creating a stunning backdrop that captivates trekkers and adventurers alike. The trek to Tilicho Lake is challenging but rewarding, offering breathtaking views, and a glimpse into the rich culture of the local communities.
